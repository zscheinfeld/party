Welcome

We’re so excited to celebrate with you! We met at summer camp, where every day was special, but there was one day of the summer that stood out from the rest. This day was literally called "Special Day," where you got to do things you didn't usually get to do. More importantly, you got to eat Chipwiches. We're bringing the Special Day and camp spirit to our wedding, Chipwiches and all, and we look forward to (hopefully) seeing you there.

Scroll down for more information. Can’t wait!

Details
Sunday, June 2

Wedding @ Woodmont Day Camp
420 Phillips Hill Rd, New City, NY 10956
12:00-4:00PM-ish

We met at summer camp so we’re bringing the summer camp spirit to our wedding. This will be a casual afternoon with lawn games, food, dancing… and even a wedding ceremony (taking place at 12:30PM so please arrive before then).

Hotel

We have discounted room rates available at the Hotel Nyack (400 High Ave, Nyack, NY 10960) that you can reserve by phone or online.

By phone: Call the hotel's front desk at (845) 675-8700 and reference the Scheinfeld/Shapiro wedding.

Online: Click here

If you don't see the date/room you want, please contact us.

Transport

There will be a shuttle pick-up from the Hotel Nyack lobby at 11:30am. Return shuttles will leave to take people back from the camp to the hotel at 4pm and 4:30pm.

Attire

We've gotten many questions about the dress code so we wanted to clarify that we are serious about dressing casually (you can indeed wear shorts!). Please wear whatever you'd be comfortable in. There will be games and some activities will take place on grass. If you're looking for a specific term to describe the dress code, in the words of our friend Shannon Carty, it's "picnic chic."
